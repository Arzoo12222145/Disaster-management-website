Crowd-sourced disaster management is harnessing the power of the public to aid in all phases of a disaster. Imagine it as a giant online collaboration platform where everyone can contribute their knowledge and skills to help during a crisis. Crowdsourcing leverages the collective knowledge and skills of the public. This can be especially helpful in emergencies where professional responders might be overwhelmed. Pooling resources, Real-time updates, Information gathering, and Communication link are some features that is inherited in crowdsourcing.
